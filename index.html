About Me

Hi, I'm Kaylee Roen. My music journey began with an electric piano my grandpa gave me for Christmas when I was a little girl. I now have studied and performed all different kinds of piano over the last 19 years. I graduated from Simpson College studying music education and taught elementary music and some strings for the last 6 years. I also love the strings family playing violin since I was in 4th grade and busting out the ukulele to sing along with my 2 sons. When I am not teaching, I love to run and spend time outside with my family.

Lessons are taught out of my home in the Copper Creek Neighborhood in Pleasant Hill, Iowa.
